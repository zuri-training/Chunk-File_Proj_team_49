ABOUT CHUNK49

Chunk49 is a website that breaks down large CSV and JSON files into smaller chunks that can be easily viewed, saved for later or downloaded. CSV and JSON files can be uploaded by authenticated users on Chunk49 website and split into smaller sizes.

Features

Break down large CSV and JSON files into smaller chunks

Files that has been broken down can be saved to view later

Chunked files can be downloaded into your device.
